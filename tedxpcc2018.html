About this Project:

Portland Community College had the opportunity to host the 2nd TEDxPCC at PCC Sylvania Campus.

TEDxPCC 2018's theme was Collective Genius which aimed to spark conversations and ignite positive community actions by bringing together community representatives and showcasing new ideas from local, regional and national voices across diverse disciplines and background. It also encouraged the concept of collectively with our knowledge and ideas, we are all genius.

We explored different abstract concepts of what it means become a collective genious.
